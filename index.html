About This Project

I grew up loving airplanes, and I wanted to create a website to share some of my favorite planes with others. This is a simple gallery of some of my favorites. From the Queen of the skies to the reliable Cessna 172, this website celebrates the beauty and innovation of airplanes. Whether you're an aviation enthusiast or just appreciate the marvels of flight, I hope you enjoy exploring this collection of amazing aircraft.

King Air B200

The King Air B200 is a versatile turboprop aircraft known for its reliability and performance, my favorite private jet for its spacious cabin and excellent handling characteristics.

Boeing 747-800

The Boeing 747-800 aka "The Queen of the Skies" is a large wide-body commercial aircraft known for its distinctive hump and impressive cargo capacity. This is the plane that Elder Uchtdorf flew, and the same one I flew home in from my mission.

Cessna 172M

The Cessna 172M is a single-engine, four-seat light aircraft known for its durability and ease of maintenance. It's a popular choice for flight training and recreational flying. N92855 is the plane that I flew to get my PPL, it will always have a special place in my heart.
